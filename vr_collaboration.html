Collaborative virtual environment in XR 27 Luglio, 2020

Coursework cross-platform application made for my master thesis in Computer Graphic at Polytechnic of Turin and at TechGraph in Rio De Janeiro. Two users, one in AR and one in VR, collaborate to place 3D objects inside their correspondant transparent shapes with the fixed rotation, translation and scale.Â  Once they're done, one of them will push a red button that will show the score achieved based on the accuracy with which they positioned the artifacts, and will move them to the next level.

Target devices:

  • Android cellphones
  • Oculus Rift, Rift S and Vive

 

Technology used:

  • Client: Unity 3D
  • Multiplayer backend system: C# .Net
  • Steam VR SDK
  • Vuforia AR SDK
  • Blender

Art pieces:

  • 3D model taken from sketchfab

In the video we can see the third level of the application, where an user in VR is helping another user in AR to move the various groceries in the correct trunks, basing their choises on the higher quantitative of protein or carbs of the element.
